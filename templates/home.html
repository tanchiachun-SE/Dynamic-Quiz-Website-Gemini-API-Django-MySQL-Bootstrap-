Welcome to Dynamic Quiz System

A Dynamic Quiz System is an advanced assessment tool designed to manage and adapt the delivery of questions to students based on their performance. Unlike traditional fixed or random quiz systems, the dynamic mechanism ensures that the difficulty level of questions aligns with the student's understanding. For instance, if a student answers correctly, the system progresses to more challenging questions, while incorrect answers lead to easier ones, providing a personalized and engaging learning experience.

The integration of Gemini AI enhances the system's capabilities by assisting lecturers in creating diverse, high-quality questions tailored to different difficulty levels and topics. Gemini AI analyzes course content and generates questions that align with learning objectives, saving educators time and effort while maintaining the quality of assessments.
